COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about SpaceFlow.

How does the 14-day trial work?

Our 14-day trial gives you full access to all features of your selected plan. No credit card is required to start. You'll receive a notification three days before your trial ends with options to continue or cancel. If you choose to continue, you can select your preferred plan and payment method.

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, the new features will be available immediately and you'll be billed the prorated difference. When downgrading, the change will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We employ industry-leading security measures including end-to-end encryption, regular security audits, and compliance with GDPR, HIPAA, and other regulations. Your data is stored in SOC 2 compliant data centers with redundant backups. We never share or sell your information to third parties.

What integrations do you support?

SpaceFlow connects with over 200 popular tools and services including Slack, Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, Salesforce, HubSpot, Jira, Asana, QuickBooks, and many more. We also offer a robust API for custom integrations with your proprietary systems.

Do you offer training and support?

Yes, all plans include access to our comprehensive knowledge base, tutorial videos, and community forums. Professional and Enterprise plans include personalized onboarding sessions, regular check-ins, and priority support. Enterprise customers also receive dedicated account management and custom training programs.

Can I access SpaceFlow on mobile devices?

Yes, SpaceFlow is fully accessible on mobile devices through our responsive web application and native apps for iOS and Android. The mobile experience includes all essential features, allowing you to stay productive even when you're away from your desk.
